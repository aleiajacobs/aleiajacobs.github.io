My Experience 

As a film production student at Chapman University's Dodge College of Film and Media Arts, I have had the opportunity to work on over 25 student productions, ranging from senior thesis films to independents. I have worked in every department on set, but script supervisor and DIT are the roles I enjoy the most as an editor. I have edited ten projects while at Chapman and have interned at two production companies thus far. I am also proficient at a variety of programs. Feel free to take a look at my resume on my About page for more in depth details about my experience and qualifications.
