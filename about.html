About Me

Based in Orange County, California I am a freelance video editor and full time student. I have edited 10+ films while at Chapman thus far and have a genuine love for the art of visual storytelling and the meticulousness of editing.

 

I am officially certified in Avid Media Composer 2019/2020. I have years of experience in Avid, Adobe Premiere, and Davinci Resolve, and am always eager to learn new programs. My journey in film began at the age of eight when I received an old digital camera from a family friend and began to make stop motion videos of my American Girl dolls to post on YouTube. At an early age I became comfortable making content for an audience.

 

In the past year I have written, directed, and edited my own short film called "Luz" as well as edited ten other independent or student films, and participated in four internships at independent production companies around the world. Through these amazing opportunities I have learned so much about the entertainment industry, visual storytelling, and myself as an editor.

 

Over the past 10+ years my love for filmmaking has only evolved and I truly believe I can bring passion and creativity to any project.
